White Mountaineering

Category

Art Direction | Layout Design

Date

Summer / Winter 2013

  • Share it:

Challenge

Being an international label originating in Japan required a design that was dynamic enough to be available in multiple languages.

Solution

The Issue was designed as an end of year annual and lookbook. This gave some additional direction when developing a color scheme and graphic identity. Using cartographics in a subdued manner reinforced both the rigidity and strenght of Mountains and the grid employed in the layout.

Result

This was a pet project for me, one that combined my love of layout design with my equally great admiration and appreciation of fashion. White Mountaineering is one of the worlds leading brands.

Platinum ABQ

Category

Web Design | Advertising

Date

Winter 2014

  • Share it:

Challenge

The Client had created a very modern luxury apartment complex in the heart of Albequrques hippest district, Nob Hill. Their digital and market presence needed to relfect the standard of living they represented. The main challenge was to create an a site that would allow potential residents to shop and apply for apartments of their choosing.

Solution

The build decided on was a web app that would allow the user to interact with the building and choose which unit best matched their needs.

Result

The site is a very linear story. A user traverses through what a day for a Platinum resident would be, then is presented with their options for which apartments suit them, and finally an application.

Stunning. I love the mountains and sunset in Abq and Platinum provides a mesmerizing view. You capture the best asset of all.

ABQ Resident
